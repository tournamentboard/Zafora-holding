About Zafora Holding
Zafora Holding is a U.S.-based strategic infrastructure, investment, and consulting company established in January 2025 and headquartered in Tampa.
The company was founded with a vision to bridge global opportunities through infrastructure intelligence, strategic partnerships, technology-driven solutions, and international business development. Zafora Holding focuses on supporting public and private sector initiatives by connecting innovation, operational strategy, and scalable development opportunities across emerging and developed markets.
Our organization is built around the belief that the future of infrastructure, technology, logistics, energy, communications, and smart development requires trusted partnerships, transparency, strategic execution, and long-term vision.
Zafora Holding is actively positioning itself to support opportunities across:


Africa


North America

Emerging global markets


We are particularly focused on helping governments, enterprises, contractors, investors, and development organizations identify and execute projects that improve connectivity, infrastructure, operations, sustainability, and economic growth.
